Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

How do I book a cab?

You can book a cab by filling out the enquiry form on our website, calling us directly, or sending us a message on WhatsApp.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a small fee.

Do you offer self-drive cars?

Currently, we only provide chauffeur-driven car rental services to ensure maximum comfort and safety for our guests.

Are fuel and driver allowance included?

For outstation trips, fuel is included in the per km rate. Driver allowance is charged separately per day. Tolls and parking are extra.

Is it safe for solo travelers?

Absolutely. All our vehicles are GPS-tracked and our drivers are background-verified to ensure a safe journey for everyone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. You can pay at the end of the trip or prepay online.
